I build digital products end to end: the web app and its infrastructure, the AI that runs inside it, and the legal and operational groundwork to actually launch it. Most of that work is product and client code. The pieces worth sharing are open-sourced below, including a toolkit to help preserve a vulnerable language.

## Start here

- 🕯️ **[abkhaz-language-open](https://github.com/1977-code/abkhaz-language-open)** - open, reproducible toolkit for learning and preserving Abkhaz (аҧсуа бызшәа), a Northwest Caucasian language UNESCO lists as vulnerable: a CC0 corpus-frequency pipeline, an A0–A2 course, and a bilingual site.
- 🪶 **[freeloader](https://github.com/1977-code/freeloader)** - Claude Code MCP + skill that offloads cheap, bulk subtasks to free local LLMs, so you stop spending premium tokens on grunt work.
- ⚖️ **[due-diligence-ru](https://github.com/1977-code/due-diligence-ru)** - pre-launch technical, legal, and readiness review for digital projects under Russian law.

## What I work on

Full-stack web apps and sites: e-commerce and marketplaces, ticketing and events platforms, dashboards, community and social products. TypeScript, React/Next.js, Node, SQLite/Postgres.

### AI, agents & LLM tooling
MCP servers, local LLM routing and cost optimization, Claude Code skills, and agent systems. Wiring models into real products, not demos.

### Design & UX
Interface and product design, with attention to how a thing actually feels to use.

### Language preservation & open data

### RU launch ops
The unglamorous part: the documents, taxes, and compliance a digital product needs to go live in Russia.
